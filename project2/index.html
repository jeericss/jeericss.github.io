Does your religion make you intolerant?

By QuickBeam | Data from WVS | Analytic Trail 1 | Analytic Trail 2

?
In this visualization I investigate the possible correlation between religiousness and the ability to justify certain dilemmas in life for 30 countries around the world. The first variable gives whether the people of each country consider themselves religious or not. The other 7 variables represents different dilemmas in life, and the ability to justify these, on a scale from 1 to 10. 1 = "Never Justifiable" and 10 = "Always Justifiable". You can brush the data by marking different parts on the variable scales. You can also edit the order of the variables, to see different correlations and patterns even better.
